Samosa

A crispy and spicy Indian snack filled with potatoes and peas.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled green peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour with water and knead into a dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, potatoes, peas, and spices. Mix well.
  3. Divide dough into small balls and roll them into thin circles.
  4. Cut each circle in half, form a cone, and fill with the potato mixture.
  5. Seal the edges with water and deep fry until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney!
