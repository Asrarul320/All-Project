Biryani

Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g Chicken (or Mutton)
  • 1 large Onion (sliced)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 cup Coriander and Mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan, add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken, tomatoes, yogurt, and spices. Cook until tender.
  4. Boil rice separately until 70% cooked.
  5. Layer rice and chicken mixture in a pot. Cook on low heat for 15 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander and mint. Serve hot!
