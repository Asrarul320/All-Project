Chicken Do Piyaza

A flavorful and spicy chicken curry made with double the onions for a rich taste.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (bone-in or boneless)
  • 4 large onions (2 chopped, 2 sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and chopped onions. Saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Stir in tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add chicken pieces and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Mix in yogurt and cook for another 10 minutes on medium heat.
  6. Add sliced onions and garam masala, then cook until chicken is fully done.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
