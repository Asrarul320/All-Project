Gajar Ka Halwa

A classic Indian dessert made with fresh carrots, milk, sugar, and dry fruits.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups grated carrots
  • 2 cups full cream milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup khoya (optional, for richness)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 10-12 cashews (chopped)
  • 10-12 almonds (chopped)
  • 10-12 raisins

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add grated carrots. Saut√© for 5-7 minutes on low flame.
  2. Add milk and cook until the carrots soften and the milk reduces.
  3. Mix in sugar and stir continuously until it dissolves.
  4. Add khoya and mix well for a richer taste.
  5. Sprinkle cardamom powder and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Garnish with cashews, almonds, and raisins.
  7. Serve warm or chilled.
